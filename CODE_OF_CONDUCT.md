# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project quality

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security report or contact maintainers via repository owner profile.
